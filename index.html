Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining DevConf 2026.

When and where is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 will take place on October 14–15, 2026 at the International Convention Center, Dhaka.

Who should attend this conference?

Developers, software engineers, students, startup founders, designers, DevOps professionals, and anyone passionate about technology.

Will workshop seats be limited?

Yes. Workshop seats are limited and available on a first-come, first-served basis.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. Pro ticket holders receive lifetime access to all recorded sessions after the conference.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Yes. Tickets can be transferred up to 7 days before the event.

How can I contact the organizers?

You can contact us through our contact form or email support@devconf2026.com.
